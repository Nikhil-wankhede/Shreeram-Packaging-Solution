Introduction to Shreeram Packaging Solutions

Shreeram Packaging Solutions is a fully automated unit of corrugated box manufacturing. Our working motto is to add care through packaging. Our products, while being utilitarian and robust, are healthy to use and be around, and environment friendly. We make our premium services available for our clients at the best prices in the industry.

Being the best-corrugated box manufacturing facility in and around central india is a matter of great pride for Shreeram Packaging Solutions. We have over a decade of robust working experience behind us and aim to become among the leading packaging solution providers in pan India.

The key operating features of Shreeram Packaging Solutions include quality that always supersedes client expectations and timely deliveries. We started with humble beginnings but now we are the regular vendors of corrugated boxes for premier Industries in central India region.

We value customer relationships above all else and never mind going that extra mile to meet client requirements. We are the best point of contact for meeting all custom requirements from corrugated boxes. Our services include creating corrugated boxes for garment export, liquor, steel utensils, toys, printers, pharmaceuticals, confectionery, engineering, and FMCG.

The rapid advancements taking place in Solution Integration across the manufacturing field give rise to a requirement for seamless business processes. This should cover the entire value chain for an organization and encompass clients, suppliers, partners, and employees. At Shreeram Packaging Solutions, our services aid clients with the identification, development, and implementation in the finest of ways. They are empowered to come up with the best fit solution that equips them to upkeep their business and product standards across the market.

We offer an end-to-end packaging solution for our clients. This initiates for using premium quality of paper and paying utmost heed to accurate paper combinations. Most items at your favorite supermarket, discount store, or shopping mall were safely delivered in boxes made of corrugated cardboard, and many are displayed in the same boxes, which were manufactured so they could be opened and used for this purpose. Corrugated cardboard is a stiff, strong, and light - weight material made up of three/five/ seven layers of brown Kraft paper. No other packaging material offers the benefits of corrugated boxes. It maximizes protection while being space efficient; it is outstandingly versatile and has a fast design - to - delivery turn around. Add to this the economic benefits arising from increased production efficiency in the industry, and you can see why corrugated packaging is hard to beat. A vast programmer of research and development is leading to more economic products and more responsive customer service. Above all the industry listens whether you need to improve food preservation, protect electronic devices, or find more economic ways to distribute and store products; the corrugated packaging industry continues to discover new and surprising answers. These are the key reasons why corrugated packaging is so popular. Wherever it can be used, it usually is more than two thirds of the world’s retailed commodities are packed and transported in it - more than all other types of packaging put together.

Our packaging boxes are 100% bio-degradable. They are created only using the best practices in the industry and do not become a breeding ground for allergens.

The primary commitment of Shreeram Packaging Solutions is to ensure that we offer a safe and healthy work environment for all our employees. This furthermore keeps our visitors and contractors healthy when they visit our manufacturing facility.

Our health and safety policy is multi-dimensional and holistic, and involves the following measures:
